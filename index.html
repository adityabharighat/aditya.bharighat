01 — About

A bit about me.

I'm a Technical Lead at Moback Technologies, where I've spent the last 9 years growing from intern to leading architecture decisions across 10+ production applications.

My focus is the JavaScript/TypeScript ecosystem — NestJS on the backend, Angular and React on the frontend — with deep work in API design, database optimization, micro-frontend architecture, and building production-ready AI features.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me hiking in the Western Ghats, photographing wildlife, playing the ghatam, or smashing a shuttle.
